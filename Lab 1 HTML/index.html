My Favorite Thing: Moon Jellyfish

Moon jellyfish are one of the most common and widely distributed species of jellyfish in the world. They are found in oceans all over the world, from the surface to depths of up to 100 meters. Moon jellyfish are known for their distinctive appearance, with a translucent, bell-shaped body that is typically about 25-40 centimeters in diameter. They have four long, trailing tentacles that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters.
